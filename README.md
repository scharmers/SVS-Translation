# SVS-Translation
Fan translations for Summer Vacation Scramble, for use with [XUnity Auto Translator](https://github.com/bbepis/XUnity.AutoTranslator)

# What is this fork?

These are edited ML translations of dialogue text and eventually h-scene text.

# Why redo the MTL?

The original translations are raw MTL. I review each line from the original and run them individually through MTL again the decide what's actually being said. I've been doing this long enough that I can recognize both MTL "phrases" as well as visually ID Japanese phrases... plus I'm familiar with the usual eroge trope dialogue to figure out what's going on. Most of the time, I can redo the original MTL translation to be much more readable and more accurate to the original Japanese. I also do searches to try to translate colloquialisms and such instead of literal translations, with varying degrees of success. On rare occasions, I will just pull something out of the blue that I feel fits the "intention" when I can't figure out a line.

# Why do this when there's native Japanese-speaking translators out there?

Honestly, it's for my own use, because I don't like reading raw MTL word salad. Plus, it takes a while before Illusion games gather enough interest to get a native text translation. KKS, for example, took 5 years. In the meantime, my translation of it (thousands and thousands of lines and months of work) was the "official" dialogue and H-scene translation in the community KKS patch.

# LOL, phrase "x" is laughably wrong!

I am by no means perfect and again I am not a native Japanese speaker. If you let me that I completely screwed up a line, let me know, and I'll fix it. I am aware that pronouns are all over the place and I'm trying to fix those.
